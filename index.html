About Me

Hello! I'm Farhoud Jafari, a postdoctoral researcher at the Carleton Univesity, specializing in vehicular cloud networks, AI and edge computing. I have a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering and a deep passion for exploring innovative solutions in intelligent transportation systems and cloud computing.

Over the years, I've held several impactful roles. I served as the Manager of the Artificial Intelligence Lab (AI Lab) at MTN Irancell, where I led cutting-edge R&D initiatives. As a Senior Enterprise Architect, I contributed to large-scale projects involving enterprise solutions for different industries. My experience also includes roles as a Senior Software Analyst and Technical Manager for enterprise architecture projects. These positions have honed my skills in research, development, and leadership, helping me bridge academic insights with practical applications.
